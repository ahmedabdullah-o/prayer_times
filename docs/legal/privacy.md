# Privacy Policy – Qibla

**Last updated:** 2026-01-22

## 1. Information We Collect

### a) Location Data  
Our app collects and stores **location data** (such as city, country, or coordinates) in order to determine accurate prayer times for your current location.

This data is stored **locally on your device only**.

### b) Analytics Data  
We use third-party analytics tools to collect **anonymous usage data**, such as:
- App interactions
- Feature usage
- Crash reports
- Device information (OS version, app version)

All the data we collect relate only to the OS version and app version.

We do not collect user identifiable data.

This data helps us identify bugs and improve app performance.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the collected data to:
- Calculate and display prayer times.
- Monitor app stability and performance.
- Fix bugs and improve user experience.

---

## 3. Data Sharing

We may share **anonymous usage data** with trusted third-party analytics providers for diagnostic and performance purposes.

We do **not** share your personal identity or sell your data.

---

## 4. Data Storage

- Location data is stored **only on your device**.
- Analytics data is processed and stored by third-party providers according to their own privacy policies.

---

## 5. Third-Party Services

The app may use:
- Posthog
- Sentry

These services may collect and process data in accordance with their own privacy policies.

---

## 6. Permissions

The app requests location permission only to determine prayer times.  
Analytics data is collected automatically but does not include personally identifiable information.

---

## 7. Data Deletion

You can:
- Clear app data or uninstall the app to remove locally stored information.

Analytics data is collected in an aggregated and anonymous form.  
We do not store data that can directly identify individual users.

---

## 8. Children’s Privacy

This app does not knowingly collect personal data from children under 13.

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected in this document.
